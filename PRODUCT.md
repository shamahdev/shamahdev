# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors to shamah.dev — recruiters, potential clients, collaborators, and fellow developers. They want to quickly understand who Shaddam is, what he builds, and whether his skills and experience align with their needs. They should leave with a clear sense of his craft, personality, and capabilities, and feel compelled to reach out.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that reflects Shaddam Amru Hasibuan's identity as a Frontend Product Engineer. Not just a resume — a living showcase of technical ability, design sensibility, and professional journey. Success means a visitor finishes with a stronger impression of the person behind the work, not just a list of jobs.

## Brand Personality

Confident, warm, precise.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** The interface itself should demonstrate frontend craft. Every animation, layout choice, and interaction should reinforce the builder's skill.
2. **Confident warmth.** Authoritative in voice and execution, but approachable in tone and detail. Technical rigor without coldness.
3. **Color with purpose.** Color is the primary differentiator — use it to guide attention, express personality, and break away from the default dark-portfolio palette. Every color choice earns its place.
4. **Precision in craft.** No accidental spacing, no default gaps, no placeholder content. Each section is deliberately composed.
5. **Narrative, not inventory.** Work experience and projects are presented as a story of growth and impact, not a chronological dump.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as a baseline. Scroll animations should respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
